The process for legally changing your name and gender marker is different in every state, this website has more information on each state's policies

Information on New York's name change laws can be found here

Here is a list of things to change once you legally change your name and/or gender:

  • Social security card (more information)
  • License
  • Passport
  • State ID
  • Bank accounts
  • Credit and debit cards
  • Post office
  • IRS
  • Voting
  • Immigration documents
  • Loans
  • Car registration
  • Employer
  • Insurance
  • Doctors' offices
  • Airlines/transportation
  • Selective Service (more information)
  • Birth certificate (Some states will not allow you to change the gender on your birth certificate)
  • Vassar

Don't discard your old ID immediately, you might need it to get new ones!

If you want a lawyer to look over it, some courts have walk-in hours
